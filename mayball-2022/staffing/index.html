JESUS MAY BALL 2022 - STAFFING

Jesus College May Ball (2022) is looking for committed and passionate individuals to work and help make this year's Ball a night to remember !!

There are a variety of roles available, whether it be working as an Entertainment Runner, Security Steward or on Food and Drinks, there is certainly a role for you!! If leadership is your calling then why not apply for the more demanding and rewarding roles of Team Leader or Court Supervisor.

Unlike some other Balls, Jesus does not operate under a 'half-on, half off' employment policy. All workers must work the whole ball, and are offered a competitive rate for this.

We aim for our applicants to willingly work as part of a team, demonstrating a readiness to embrace their tasks and immerse themselves within the Ball's atmosphere. An army of charming workers will help make the 20th of June an exceptional night. Prior May Ball or hospitality experience is valuable, but not necessary.

This is a night to remember and a fabulous opportunity to gain experience and to earn money whilst working at one of Cambridge's most anticipated and contested events of the year !!

If this is for you, please apply before January 20th, when applications close, for first consideration. Interviews will take place on February 11th, 12th and 13th.

To apply, please fill in the online application form below.

Please feel free to contact Mojola and Orchid with any queries or further questions via staffing@jesusmayball.com

Team Leader (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

Team Leaders will need to effectively manage their respective teams, clearly communicating with members and ensuring that everyone can confidently carry out their roles.

We are looking for people who:

  • Have amazing communication skills
  • Work well under pressure
  • Raise spirits, motivate and manage their team
  • Can use their initiative and be proactive

Prior leadership experience is necessary for this role. Team Leaders will also have 'right to buy' tickets to the next Jesus May Ball.

Court Supervisor (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

Court Supervisors ensure the smooth operation of their assigned court or area. This includes communicating with Team Leaders and any relevant committee members to help solve any issues when they arise or to send for extra staff when required. As a Court Supervisor you are also responsible for the signing in and out of workers at the beginning and end of their shifts/breaks.

We are looking for people who:

  • Work well under pressure
  • Raise spirits, motivate and manage their team
  • Have amazing communication skills
  • Can use their initiative and be proactive

Prior leadership experience is necessary for this role. Team Leaders will also have 'right to buy' tickets to the next Jesus May Ball.

Food and Drinks Worker (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

Food and drinks workers will rotate through a range of positions throughout the night. These positions include the preparation and serving of food and drink, as well as the replenishment of all stalls.

We are looking for people who:

  • Are friendly and welcoming
  • Can maintain professionalism when interacting with guests
  • Listen well when instructed
  • Can multitask and ensure the tidiness of stalls whilst also serving
  • Can work well under pressure and adapt quickly to new tasks

Prior hospitality or catering experience is beneficial but not necessary.

Security Steward (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

Security Stewards must report anyone attempting to enter the Ball without a valid ticket, however they will never be expected to directly confront or single handedly remove any unauthorised persons. You will also be responsible for guest health and safety, which includes fire stewarding and reporting any inappropriately or dangerously behaving individuals.

We are looking for people who:

  • Are attentive and hard-working
  • Can multitask
  • Can clearly communicate, especially when assistance is needed
  • Can interact professionally with guests

Entertainment (Ents) Runners (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

Ents runners need to escort all acts to the correct stages at the correct times, ensuring that the night's entertainment timetable runs smoothly.

We are looking for people who:

  • Are friendly and can maintain a degree of professionalism when interacting with acts
  • Can maintain their calm under pressure
  • Have great time management and organisational skills

On-Call Team (General Worker) (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

On-Call workers will be assigned a variety of responsibilities throughout the night, depending on where manpower is needed. This role is one of the most exciting as it's so diverse, but you must be easily adaptable and able to stay calm under pressure. Please note that as a 'General Worker' you will receive a timetable and be allocated different roles, rather than being on the 'Hit Squad', which is slightly different.

We are looking for people who:

  • Can use their initiative and be proactive
  • Respond well to direction
  • Can work well under pressure
  • Can multitask and take on a variety of tasks

Litter Collection Worker (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

The Litter Collection team will collect rubbish throughout the night and ensure that it is correctly disposed of. During the night workers will rotate to different areas, allowing you to experience as much of the Ball as possible. This is an essential role as a tidy and clean Ball will add to the guest experience, making their night run as smoothly as possible. Litter collection during the Ball will help minimise excess clear-up once the night has finished.

We are looking for people who:

  • Can maintain a level of professionalism when working in close proximity to guests
  • Are attentive
  • Are hardworking

Clear-up Worker (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

Clear-up workers are expected to help take down Ball decorations/furniture as well as clearing any remaining litter from the previous night. All Clear-up workers must not have gone out the night before.

We are looking for people who:

  • Work efficiently and effectively
  • Can undertake slightly physically demanding tasks (e.g. moving chairs etc.)

Set-up Worker (Starting at £9.90* Per Hour)

Set-up workers will aid the setting up of the Ball during the afternoon prior to the event itself.

We are looking for people who:

  • Have a great attention to detail
  • Work efficiently
  • Can follow direction

Important Information:

Please be aware that Jesus College May Ball does not operate on a 'half-on, half-off' employment system. If your application is successful we will contact you and you will be invited for an interview to provide further information about your role. The shift length for standard workers is 10 hours (including a 1 hour site tour) plus two unpaid 30 minute breaks. The shift length for Court Supervisors and Team Leaders is 11 hours (including a 1 hour site tour) plus two unpaid 30 minute breaks. 2 hours of onsite training must be attended at Jesus College. We will host different sessions with the compulsory attendance of all workers at one of them. The shift length for both Set-up and Clear-up workers is 6 hours and Clear-up workers must not have gone out the night before. All workers at Jesus College May Ball will be required to read a contract (which stipulates the terms and conditions of employment), sign it and return it to us. We will also require a passport photo of each worker and two £75 cheques made payable to "Jesus College May Ball". These are to cover any minor or major breaches of contract but will otherwise be destroyed after the Ball on fulfilment of the employment.
