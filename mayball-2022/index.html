TAKING FLIGHT

JESUS COLLEGE MAY BALL 2022

Taking Flight - Jesus College May Ball 2022

Have you ever dreamt of taking flight? Wished that, just for one day, you might grow wings and soar amongst the birds? Or that, if only for a moment, you might take the place of the planets and the stars to marvel at the dynamic beauty of our cosmos?

This June, bringing the clouds and stars to the ground, the Jesus College May Ball will allow you to take flight. You will have the chance to rise through the atmosphere, untethered, to drift amongst the clouds. A warming cradle of the wind and air will lift you higher than you ever may have hoped, taking you through and beyond our atmosphere to be amongst the stars.

So, join us on the 20th June 2022 to experience ‘Taking Flight’.

FAQs

How many guest tickets can I buy?

On Launch Night (in the Bar) and from 13:00 on the 24th February, Jesuans will be able to buy their own ticket and ballot for up to two guest tickets. On Sunday 27th February, winners of the ballot will receive an email requesting payment. Tickets then go on sale to the rest of the university in early March, should any remain.

I can't make the Ball anymore - what should I do?

The Jesus College May Ball doesn't offer refunds - however, it is possible to transfer the ticket into another name, although there is a fee for this (the majority of which is donated to charity!). We may also have a waitlist of people who are looking to buy a ticket from someone - tickets sold to the waiting list will be subject to a significantly reduced admin fee - please contact us at ticketing@jesusmayball.com. We forbid guests from selling tickets for a profit, and would like to urge those who are looking for a ticket to apply through the waitlist first to avoid high markups. If you suspect you have been offered a ticket at a markup, let us know as we will cancel tickets sold at above market rate.

I'm on the waiting list… now what?

If any spare tickets become available (e.g. from people not paying for their tickets), we will contact people on the waiting list in the order they went on. You'll stay on the waiting list until a few weeks before the Ball, to give you the best chance of getting a ticket.

Can I change the name on my ticket?

Name changes on tickets can be done here, for a fee of £40, however this fee will increase to £50 after the 22nd April. Changes will be made to the tickets after payment confirmation. The deadline for all name change requests is the 6th June. Tickets sold to the waiting list will be subject to a £10 name change fee, rising to £20 after the 22nd April.
