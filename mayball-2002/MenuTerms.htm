Terms and conditions

 

Please note that the tickets are strictly non-transferable and non-refundable save in circumstances deemed to be sufficiently exceptional by the Jesus College May Ball Committee ("the Committee"). The Committee reserves the right to refuse any application for tickets. In addition, the Committee reserves the right to refuse entry to the May Ball to any ticket-holder or to require any ticket-holder to leave the May Ball, where the Committee and/or its duly authorised representatives have reasonable grounds to believe that that ticket-holder may have committed or may be about to commit a criminal offence or has engaged or is engaging in other such activities as the Committee deems unacceptable. The Committee reserves the right to conduct searches of any ticket-holder in order to verify that the grounds for its belief exist. Furthermore, the Committee reserves the right to make reasonable alterations to the programme of the May Ball without notice to ticket-holders. All decisions of the Committee are final. Queries should be made either by post to Josephine Armstrong, May Ball Tickets, Jesus College, Cambridge, CB5 8BL, or email to mayball-tickets@jesus.cam.ac.uk
