Jesus May Ball, 2010

Mr. Hudson

Since signing to Kanye West's record label GOOD Music, the Birmingham-born singer
has enjoyed hits with "Supernova" and "White Lies", as well as massive collaborations
such as the international smash "Forever Young".
Fresh from a "Best Breakthrough Act" win at the Q Awards, and a support slot on Jay-Z's
UK tour, Hudson's headline set promises to be unmissable.
www.myspace.com/mrhudson



DJ Sub Focus

An indispensable part of the British drum'n'bass scene, Sub Focus has scored three
number ones in the UK Dance chart, as well as the recent radio smashes "Splash" and
"Could This Be Real". The Surrey-based DJ is sure to bring an earth-shattering headline
set to the DJ tent.
www.myspace.com/subfocus



Two Door Cinema Club

This Banger trio has dominated the Irish music sceme for years and is not set to take
the world by storm, recently releasing their debut album 'Tourist History' to great success.
Signed to Kitsune music, and set to embark on a world tour including Glastonbury and
Reading Festival, be sure to catch them at this stop in Cambridge. Their hits such as
"I Can Talk" and "Something Good Can Work" are set to send anyone present into a dancing frenzy.
www.myspace.com/twodoorcinemaclub



The Fillers

As recommended and lauded by Brandom Flowers of the Killers, the Fillers are the best tribute
band in the country, perfectly recreating the stage show and the sound of the multi-Platinum
American superstars.
www.myspace.com/thefillersrock

Clean Bandit

Clean Bandit, a collaboration between the Chatto Quartet, Jonwandeck and MC Ssegamic,
have created a precision blend of hip hop, electro(nica) and classical music. They have
played sold-out gigs and festivals across the country both as a band and in their three
separate entities.
www.myspace.com/cleanbandit

Minnaars

Minnaars is an Indie, Math Rock and Dance-Punk band from Leicester. They've been around
since 2008 and are well on the road to success, having played at Reading and Leeds Festivals
and on a European Tour. Their debut album is expected later this year.
www.myspace.com/minnaars
