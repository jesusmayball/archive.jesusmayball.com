Jesus May Ball Committee invites you to spend a night in Never Never Land, where dreams are born and time is never planned, on 19th June 2006. Inspired by J. M. Barrie's classic book, the ball promises to be a magical evening.

With kind permission of the Great Ormond Street Children's Hospital, the ideas and fantasy of a timeless land will be set against the stunning grounds of Jesus College.
