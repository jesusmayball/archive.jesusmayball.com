Ents

The entertainment at the Jesus May Ball has never disappointed, and this year will be no exception. Last years’ stars included the hugely successful instrumental duo Nizlopi, jazz singer Tyler James, and Kings of Queen, the popular tribute band.

This year the ents are sure to please the crowd. From the high profile live acts in the main marquee, to the dedicated jazz and acoustic areas for quiet moments, and the fun fair, there will be no time to get bored!

If you are interested in performing at the ball, please get in touch with the ents team, Kate O’Brien, Mark Fletcher, and James Warland, at mayball-ents@jesus.cam.ac.uk.
